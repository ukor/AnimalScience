Philosophy

The department offers courses which have been designed to develop scientist with a broad knowlegde of the fundamental and applied science, common to all farm animals and thier products, as well as the principles of animal production. The department caters for the brand of agriculture concerned with teaching of courses such as animal nutrition, anatomy and physiology, health and diseases, animal production and management, genetics and other biotechnological courses relevant to animal production. The programe is also design to train student in areas of pasture production and range mangement

Objective

The overall objective of the programme is to train students to acquire skills and competence required for service as animal scientist. Graduate of the programme will serve as a major source of manpower for the livestock industry, goverment ministries and international organisations, which emphasize animal production and mangement, goverment and private firms, universities and other educational and research establishment with interest in animal training. Graduate of the programme are also expected to perform in all business concerns.
